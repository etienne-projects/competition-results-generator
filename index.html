  • 7:00pm -7:30 pm – Meet as a total group in the Vineyard Café for information.

Group Rules

There are two basic group rules:

Confidentiality Rule – What is shared in the group stays in the group unless permission is given to share the information outside of the group.

Conversation Rule – You can only participate in the group discussion if you did the weekly assignment. Otherwise you must just listen to others.

WEIGH IN PROCEDURE

Weigh ins will be done each Thursday from 6:30-6:55pm and 8:30-9pm. If you’re present for class, you’re expected to weigh in. You must weigh in at least 9 times during the competition to be considered for the “biggest loser.” Measures will be taken to assure that each competitor has privacy during his weigh in. The last weigh in will be March 21st, 2019. The Biggest Loser will be announced during the Grand Finale on March 28th, 2019.
